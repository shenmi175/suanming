# Cyber Fate SearXNG

This directory configures the local SearXNG container used by Cyber Fate web research.

SearXNG is licensed under AGPL-3.0-or-later. Keep this service local unless you harden the `server.secret_key`, limiter, bot protection, and network exposure settings.

Search result pages and extracted article text remain copyrighted by their original publishers. Cyber Fate uses short excerpts and model-generated summaries for research context and source citation only; do not republish full articles or bypass publisher terms.
